## Key Features

GetFramework integrates seamlessly with your existing workflows, providing real-time insights and automated scheduling that adapts to your team's needs. Our platform supports multiple franchises under a single dashboard, making it easy to maintain brand consistency while allowing local customization.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="For Franchises" icon="users">
    Manage multiple locations with centralized control and local flexibility. Create brand guidelines that ensure consistency across all marketing materials while allowing individual franchisees to customize content for their local markets.
    
    <Callout kind="info">Franchise owners can set up approval workflows to maintain quality standards across all locations.</Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="For Teams" icon="user">
    Distributed teams can collaborate in real-time with shared templates, automated scheduling, and integrated feedback systems. Our AI tools help identify the best times to post content and suggest optimizations based on performance data.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
      ```javascript
const client = new GetFramework({
  apiKey: process.env.GETFRAMEWORK_API_KEY
});

// Schedule a campaign
await client.campaigns.schedule({
  template: 'social-media-post',
  schedule: '2024-10-15T10:00:00Z',
  locations: ['store-1', 'store-2']
});
```
      ```python
from getframework import Client

client = Client(api_key=os.getenv('GETFRAMEWORK_API_KEY'))

# Create a template
campaign = client.campaigns.create(
    template_id='newsletter',
    content={'headline': 'New Product Launch'}
)
```
