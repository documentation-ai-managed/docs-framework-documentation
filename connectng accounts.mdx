title: Connecting your accounts
description: >
  Before sharing anything you will need to connect the social media accounts you
  want to publish to.
---


Framework supports Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Google Business Profile, Threads, TikTok and YouTube.

To connect an account go to **Settings**, then open the **Social Media** tab.\
Here you can see the status of existing connections and add new ones using the **Connect** button.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Framework uses GetLate to create a secure connection to your accounts. Your login details stay private. GetLate sends Framework a token once you approve access. You remain in control of the permissions you grant and you can remove access at any time.
</Callout>

### Who can connect accounts

Only someone with the correct access level on the social media platform can create the connection.\
For example, you will need manager or admin access for business pages before linking them.

### Reauthorising or removing access

If you change your password on a connected platform, the connection to Framework may need to be refreshed. Go back to **Settings** then **Social Media**, open the connection and select **Refresh Connection**.

You can also disconnect a platform here. If you disconnect a platform you will no longer be able to publish content to that account from Framework.
